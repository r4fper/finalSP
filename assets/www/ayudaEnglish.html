Login

Easy and simple you can login in the application with only enter your type of document, user, and password.

If I am not registered. Do I do?

In the initial window of the application you will find a link that you will register in the system.

If you would like to enroll in a program?

On application startup screen follow these steps:
Offer.
We are looking for the program of interest.
Select the program.
Agree to the terms.
Press the button register.

See my courses

You must be logged (see log above), in the My courses you will have access to your academic information option.

Download my certificates and attestations

To download your certificates, and/or academic records, you should choose the certificates option and enter your type and number of the document; If you already started session will need not enter your identity document.

Look up information centers

Select Centres to access everything you want to know your training center: Google Maps Geolocation.

Modify my personal information

You can check your personal information or modify from the option record that is once make the home session.

Create resume

Create your resume format SENA, by pressing the resume option bearing in mind that you should have started session.

Facial authentication

The application has an option to perform facial authentication, so a photo will be taken with the camera of the device and the system will give or deny access; Depending on if this or not, the person registered in the system.
